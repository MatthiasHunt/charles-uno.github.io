Winner of the St Stephen's Housing Services 2014 (?) chili cook-off fundraiser. 

- a pound and a half of chorizo
- 7 cups sweet potato
- 7 cups onion
- 2 cans black beans
- 7 cups tomatoes (fresh frozen!)
- 2 cups jalapenos (fresh frozen!)
- 5 big cloves garlic

serve with cheese. chipotle cheddar!
